About Me

Hey, I’m Quade — a writer passionate about telling stories that reveal something real, whether it’s about digital media, street basketball in Prague, or late-night rooftop drinks in Istanbul. I’ve studied journalism and media, and I use my writing to explore identity, culture, and connection in unexpected places.

In every piece I write, I try to keep it authentic. That means emotion, context, and a clear point of view. I believe writing isn’t just about what you say — it’s how you make people feel when they read it.
